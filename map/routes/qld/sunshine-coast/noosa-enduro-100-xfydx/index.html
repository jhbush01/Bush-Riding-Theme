About this route

gO ON... GET DIRTY AT THE one that started it all
Get ready for one hundred gruelling kilometres of the Noosa Trail network taking in some of Australia’s most scenic tracks, trails and off-road real estate.

From singletrack to fire roads, bike paths, dirt roads and fields – this course is as diverse and varied as the Hinterland itself!

Plus, with over 1500m of climbing to tackle over the 100km event, this isn’t for your occasional rider.

The challenging yet rewarding course starts and finishes at the Festival Village in Tewantin with a loop that takes you through the Noosa Hinterland with two major acscents and visits to Pomona, Cooran, and Kin Kin.

Vetted by Noosa Enduro.

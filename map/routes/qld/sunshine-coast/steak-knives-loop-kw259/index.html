About this route

With the start and finish in Pomona access by rail is a great option for those travelling to the Sunshine Coast. About half the route is paved, but with some extended gravel sections and a stretch on the Noosa Trail Network – a gravel bike is recommended. The route goes through Cooroy at about halfway – an ideal time to refuel!

About this route

Classic multi-terrain feel and some premium graded gravel roads, sealed roads, fire roads, farms, track and trails.

This course is designed 100% for gravel bikes, just be prepared, it’s not your typical gravel ride.

Just like last year, this timed gravel ride is a non-competitive event with just one category for all riders and no prizes given.

In terms of equipment:

Bikes – Gravel or Cyclo Cross bikes are perfect. You could do on a mountain bike if you wish but it will be very slow, especially on the gravel roads. E-bikes are allowed.

Beginner riders may have to dismount on a few occasions for some very short sections and as such, gravel or mountain bike shoes are strongly recommended. Experienced off-road riders shouldn’t need to during the ride as a general rule.

Tyres – bigger is better! 34mm + knobby robust tyres are still recommended
Gear ratio’s – the terrain is hilly, so gear ratios should be selected on individual ability. While not super steep, is still long so it’s recommended to go smaller if in doubt of where you’re fitness is at – especially considering the Mothar Mountain Climb up Hill Road.

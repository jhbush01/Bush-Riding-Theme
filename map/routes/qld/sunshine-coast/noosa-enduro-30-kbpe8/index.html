About this route

A SUPER SHORT ACTION-PACKED SUNDAY RIDE
This route takes a scenic lap from Tewantin out and around the Harry Springs Reserve with everything from single track to rapid downhills and majestic fire trail.

The course is still suitable for a wide variety of ages and abilities including our younger riders (minimum age is 13).

While the course terrain is varied and may be challenging for some, it’s a course that anyone can try their hand at, even if you are completely new to cycling.

It is recommended that beginners ride a mountain bike, while for the more experienced riders any off-road bike will do.
